Common Questions

Everything you need to know before signing up.

Your first leased unit is subsidized at 0% APR — no financing cost on the primary piece of equipment. If you add a second unit, a 12% APR applies to whichever unit carries the higher retail price. This structure lets us subsidize entry-point cost-to-acquire while staying viable on multi-unit deals.

Most shops are live within 48 hours of checkout. We handle the widget embed and labor-rate configuration on our end — you get a one-line embed code and a short onboarding brief. No dev required. Hardware units ship within 3–5 business days and pair automatically once connected.

No Contract plans cancel anytime with 14 days' notice. The first No Contract month also includes a full 30-day money-back guarantee — no questions asked. 12-Month contracts are binding for their full term, with early-termination provisions spelled out in the Order Form. Upfront plans are non-refundable after checkout.

All vision-model inference runs locally on the hardware unit in your shop — customer vehicle images never leave your network. SMS handoff data is encrypted in transit and stored with role-based access controls. We do not sell, share, or train on your customer data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Eligibility is checked during the hardware lease application — not at the software checkout step. Requirements: minimum 6 months in business, active commercial bank account, and a verifiable shop address. Most established independent shops qualify within 24 hours of submitting the lease form.
