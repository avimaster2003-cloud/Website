Military-Grade Isolation.

When customers upload photos of their VIN numbers or license plates, standard AI bots send them to third-party APIs. We process them locally on hardware we physically control.

100% Air-Gapped

The LLM inference engine has its outbound internet access restricted. Data enters, quotes leave, but your customer's data is never used to train a global cloud model.

Cloudflare Tunnels

All widget traffic bypasses public DNS and is routed through encrypted Cloudflare Zero Trust Tunnels directly into our secure, localized ports.

Ephemeral Memory

The context window (short-term memory) is completely wiped the moment the browser session ends. We enforce zero persistent storage of PII.

The Liability of Cloud AI

Why 99% of "AI Service Advisors" are walking data privacy lawsuits waiting to happen.

The SaaS Wrapper Way

  • Data Surrender: When a customer uploads an insurance card or VIN, that image is sent directly to OpenAI's public API servers.

  • Shadow Training: Hidden in SaaS Terms of Service is clauses allowing your shop's proprietary labor rates to train their future global models.

  • API Latency: Relying on cloud APIs means your widget breaks during their peak hours. If OpenAI goes down, your booking pipeline dies.

The Apex Edge

  • Total Sovereignty: Every token generated, every photo analyzed, and every phone number captured stays on hardware we own and control.

  • Zero Cloud Dependencies: Because the models run locally on our Mac Studio arrays, our uptime is not tethered to external AI tech companies.

  • TLS Verification: End-to-end encryption via Cloudflare means man-in-the-middle attacks on your customer chat logs are mathematically impossible.
